Responsive Column Widths

Columns can change width at different breakpoints:

• Extra small (xs): Takes 12 columns (full width)

• Medium (md): Takes 6 columns (half width)

• Large (lg): Takes 4 columns (one-third width)

• Extra small (xs): Takes 12 columns (full width)

Column Ordering

Second in HTML, First in display (order-1)
