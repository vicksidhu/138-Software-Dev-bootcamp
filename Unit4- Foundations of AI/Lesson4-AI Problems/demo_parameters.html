📊 The pool of words being considered
How Top P works: All words are sorted by probability, highest first. We keep adding words to the "pool" until their combined probability reaches our Top P value. Only words inside this pool can be picked. Higher Top P = larger pool = more surprising choices possible.
Top K — maximum word options
Only top 1 Top 8 4
📊 The word menu — which K words are allowed
How Top K works: After ranking all words by probability, only the top K are allowed. Everything else is cut off completely, no matter how probable they are. Top K=1 means the AI always picks the single most likely word — totally deterministic, no variety at all.
